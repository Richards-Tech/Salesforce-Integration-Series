# 📘 Day 3/100 – Can Salesforce Integrate with Any Application?

We've learned what Salesforce Integration is and why companies need it. Now let me answer one of the most common questions...

> **Can Salesforce integrate with any application?**  
> The answer is **YES... in most cases. ✅**

If an application exposes an **API (REST or SOAP)**, supports a **database connection**, **file exchange (CSV/XML)**, or can communicate through **middleware**, Salesforce can usually integrate with it.

---

## 🏢 Common Examples

* **ERP Systems:** SAP & Oracle ERP, Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central 💰
* **Payment Gateways:** Stripe 💳
* **Support & IT Ticketing:** Jira & ServiceNow 🎫
* **Marketing:** HubSpot & Mailchimp 📧
* **Messaging:** Slack & WhatsApp 💬

---

## 💼 Real Project Insight

In one of my projects, we integrated Salesforce with **Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central** using **Dell Boomi**.

Whenever a customer was created or updated in Salesforce, the information was automatically synchronized with Business Central—eliminating duplicate data entry and reducing manual effort for the operations team.

> **Key takeaway:** Integration isn't about connecting systems. It's about connecting business processes.

---

## 🧠 Pro Tip

Many developers ask:  
❌ *Can Salesforce integrate with this application?*

A better question is:  
✅ ***What's the best integration approach for this application?***

The answer depends on the business requirements—not just the technology.

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistake

A lot of developers assume every integration should be **real-time**.

That's not always true. Sometimes a **scheduled batch** or an **event-driven approach** is more reliable, scalable, and cost-effective.

We'll cover all of these in the coming posts!
