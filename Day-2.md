A customer places an order on your website. 🛒

## 🚀 Why Invest in Salesforce Integrations?

With the right integration, data flows automatically between systems.

```text
Website ──► Salesforce ──► ERP ──► Shipping ──► Customer
```

✨ Benefits:
No duplicate data.

No manual work.

No copy-paste errors.

Faster business processes.

💡 Key Takeaway
The goal of integration isn't just connecting systems—it's automating business.
