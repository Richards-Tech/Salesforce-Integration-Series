# 📘 Day 1/100 – What is Salesforce Integration?

Imagine your CRM is amazing... but your Finance team uses **SAP**, Support uses **Jira**, Marketing uses **HubSpot**, and your ERP is **Microsoft Dynamics**.

How do all these systems talk to each other? 🤔

---

## 👉 What is Salesforce Integration?

**Salesforce Integration** is the process of connecting Salesforce with other applications so they can share data automatically, securely, and in real time (or in batches).

---

## 🛒 Real-World Example

```text
Customer places an order on your website
                   │
                   ▼
Salesforce creates an Opportunity
                   │
                   ▼
SAP creates the Sales Order
                   │
                   ▼
The warehouse ships the product
                   │
                   ▼
Salesforce gets the tracking number automatically

```
💡 Key Takeaway
Salesforce isn't meant to do everything—it shines when it works seamlessly with the rest of your business.
